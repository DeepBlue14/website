Introduction

Welcome to my website. As an undergraduate student at the University of Massachusetts Lowell, I have the opportunity to take COMP 91.64 GUI Programming I with Dr. Wenjin Zhou. This is my implementation of Assignment #4 for that class. Since my work in Dr. Holly Yanco's lab involves human-robot interaction, I am eager to learn how to design and develop elegant and intuitive client-side web-based applications.

Education

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Massachusetts Lowell in the Computer Science program. I also serve as senior technical advisor for the robotics club.

Employment

Robotics Laboratory

I am currently employed as an Undergraduate Researcher in Dr. Holly Yanco's Robotics Laboratory at the UMass Lowell's Computer Science Department. My work includes designing and implementing experimental software for robotic systems, assisting graduate students in running their experiments, and publishing papers.

My work-related research includes tactical systems, user interfaces for assistive and Urban Search and Rescue (USAR) robots, optics, and mobile systems. Many of these projects are developed using ROS, a meta-operating system for developing robotics applications, while some of our more portable software is developed on Android.
